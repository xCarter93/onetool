**map-schema-tool.ts changes:**

The existing mapper normalizes headers and field names by removing underscores/spaces/hyphens, then does substring matching. This works for simple cases but needs enhancement for contact/property fields because:
- CSV headers like "first name" or "contact first name" should match `contact.firstName`
- CSV headers like "address" or "street" should match `property.streetAddress`
- The dot in `contact.firstName` needs to be stripped during normalization

Update the normalization to also strip dots (add `.` to the regex: `/[_\s\-?.]/g`).
